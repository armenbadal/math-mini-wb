which is 9 fewer than Bonnie has. How many cousins does Robert
have?

them in boxes of 30 books each. How many boxes did they need?

on Friday and stopped at 6 p.m. on Saturday. How many hours did Joey’s
team dance?

pumpkin that she has contains 8 cups of pumpkin. How many cakes can
she make?

Mr. Brown plowed 6 acres in 1 hour. At this rate, how long will it take him
to plow 21 acres?

Jeff left Hartford at 2:15 p.m. and arrived in Boston at 4:45 p.m. How long
did the drive take him?

The total ticket sales for a soccer game were $1,260; 210 tickets were
purchased. If all the tickets are the same price, what was the cost of a ticket?

Shane rolls a die with faces numbered 1 through 6. What is the probability
that Shane rolls a 3?

Susan traveled 114 miles in 2 hours. If she keeps going at the same rate,
how long will it take her to complete the remaining 285 miles of her trip?

Wendy has 5 pairs of pants and 7 shirts. How many different outfits can
she make with these items (each outfit consists of one pair of pants and one
shirt)?

dance. How many boys were at the dance?
